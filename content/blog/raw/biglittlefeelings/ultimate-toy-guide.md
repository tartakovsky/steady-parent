The Best Toys for Infants, Toddlers & Kids
Because finding the perfect gift for babies and kids is pretttttty much the best feeling ever. (And no - the “perfect” giftdoesn’t need to be the fanciest or most expensive. And you don’t need to get a zillion of them. Promise.) 
Here’s a beautiful reality to brighten your day: for babies, toddlers, and preschoolers, playing IS learning.
Through the act of play, kids learn essential skills, like problem solving, motor development, language, spatial skills and coordination, communication and negotiation, and creativity. And we all know that toys are a *very* important part of the playtime experience.
With that in mind, **we’ve put together this go-to holiday gift guide**! We broke it down into different age groups, because what works for a newborn might not be ideal for your six-year-old. And we’ve leaned into open-ended toys that invite your little one to do thinking, experimenting and playing - a muuuuch better option than one-and-done toys that entertain our kids (briefly, probably) and do the thinking and the playing for them.
