# Janet Lansbury (janetlansbury.com) - SEO Strategy Analysis

## Overview

Janet Lansbury is a parenting expert and author specializing in RIE (Resources for Infant Educarers) parenting philosophy. The site generates significant SEO traffic through comprehensive how-to content targeting parents searching for solutions to behavioral challenges.

**Total Content Pages Analyzed:** 215 unique URLs
**Keyword Ranking Pages:** 53 (core content with high keyword associations)

## Strategies Identified

### 1. How-To Guides (27 core pages)
**Coverage:** Behavioral challenges, discipline, emotional regulation, communication

Janet Lansbury's primary strategy is comprehensive how-to articles addressing specific parenting problems. These pages rank for searches like:
- "how to discipline 2 year olds"
- "why timeouts fail"
- "how to stop whining"
- "angry outbursts in kids"

The content positions her as a solutions-oriented expert rather than a theorist. Pages often use "what to do instead" frameworks, teaching alternatives to traditional punitive approaches.

**High-Ranking Pages:**
- "What's Too Strict, What's Not Strict Enough" (40+ keywords)
- "Angry Outbursts, Screaming and Hurtful Words" (29 keywords)
- "Ten Best Ways to Encourage Toddlers to Talk" (28 keywords)

### 2. Segment-Pages (17 core pages)
**Coverage:** Age-specific parenting (infants to school-age)

Content targets specific developmental stages with age-relevant guidance:
- Infant/newborn (0-12 months)
- Toddler (12-36 months) - heavy emphasis here
- Preschooler (3-5 years)

Examples:
- "Does My Two Year Old Need Preschool"
- "Already Exhausted by a 15-Month-Old's Behavior"
- "Age 4 Seems Like Another Planet"

This strategy captures high-intent searches from parents with specific-aged children looking for developmental guidance.

### 3. Vertical-Landing (6 pages)
**Coverage:** RIE parenting philosophy and Magda Gerber's approach

Janet Lansbury establishes authority through content on the RIE methodology (her core philosophy):
- "RIE Parenting Basics - 9 Ways to Put Respect Into Action"
- "Respect, Trust, Acceptance - Magda Gerber's Therapeutic Approach"

This positions her as the definitive voice on RIE parenting in search results and establishes a distinct methodological identity versus competitors.

### 4. Best-of-Content (3 pages)
**Coverage:** Podcast and media extensions

The Unruffled podcast extends her reach and creates additional ranking opportunities:
- "Respectful Parenting Podcasts: Janet Lansbury Unruffled"
- Podcast hub page
- Podcast episode mention pages

## Key Insights

1. **Content-Heavy Strategy** - 215 unique pages with ~1000 tracked keywords indicates extensive, long-tail content optimization
2. **Topic Cluster Authority** - Dominance in specific behavioral topics (tantrums, discipline, whining) through multiple angles
3. **Developmental Stage Targeting** - Clear segmentation by child age, capturing intent-rich searches
4. **Methodology as Moat** - RIE positioning creates differentiation vs. other parenting experts
5. **Podcast Integration** - Media presence amplifies authority and provides ranking diversity

## Competitive Advantages

- Established voice (years of content, consistent publishing)
- Depth of content on specific behavioral challenges
- Clear methodology framework (RIE) that appeals to a specific parent demographic
- Podcast integration extends reach beyond articles
