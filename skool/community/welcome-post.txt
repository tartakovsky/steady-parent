Welcome to The Steady Parent Method Community

Hey there, and welcome.

If you're here, you're probably tired of losing your cool. Tired of the guilt that follows. Tired of feeling like you're doing it wrong.

You're not doing it wrong. You're just doing it without the right tools.

---

WHAT THIS COMMUNITY IS FOR

This is a space for parents of 3-9 year olds who want to:
- Stop yelling (and actually know what to do instead)
- Set boundaries that stick—without guilt or endless negotiations
- Stay calm when your kid is anything but
- Raise emotionally healthy kids while keeping your own sanity

We're here to support each other through the messy middle of parenting.

---

HOW TO GET STARTED

1. Head to the Classroom tab to access the full course
2. Start with Module 1: The Regulated Parent (your calm is the foundation)
3. Download the Quick Reference Scripts in the Resources section
4. Come back here when you're stuck, celebrating, or just need to vent

---

COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

1. Be kind. Everyone here is doing their best with what they have.
2. Keep it confidential. What's shared here stays here.
3. No judgment, no parenting wars. We're all on the same team.
4. Ask for help. That's literally why we're here.
